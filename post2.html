Allina the Mind Reader

The Lore

Allina came from an alternate dimension different from ours. She wanted to meet humans in their world, but it all did not go as planned when a bug was found in her friend's portal program. Instead of going into the human realm, she can was transported into the internet browsers where any humans can find her. To test her communications skills, she asks whoever is behind the screen a number between 1 to 1000.

*I drew a comic on what happened to Allina

How did It start

So, this was actually the second homework assignment I had to do in my first Java class. I thought it would be a fun little project to implement this into JavaScript. It took me almost 2-3 hours just to get some things to work. The buttons and layouts costed a lot of my time since I wanted to have a good UI, but it was a worth it. I also wanted a character to guess your number just like how Akinator guesses your character, but most of the characters were licensed. I drew an original one.

The Model

Translating JavaScript whilst only knowing Java was not as difficult as I thought. The only problem I had to overcome was the idea of the "browser compiler".
